About Us

Going by the name of Space Technology Students’ Society (spAts), this student body is the functional unit of Kalpana Chawla Space Technology Cell (KCSTC), which is the contact point of ISRO at IIT Kharagpur. This student group has conceived and bred the only fest in India that is solely dedicated to space technology, National Students’ Space Challenge (NSSC). NSSC saw the light of day in 2011 when it saw a prolific response from all over India in its very first year. In 2012 the fest witnessed an exponential growth in student participation. This year NSSC plans to widen its reach even further.
